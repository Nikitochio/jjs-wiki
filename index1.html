Base Moveset

[1] - Stockpile

Unblockable, Bypasses Ragdoll, Cancellable

Cooldown: 12 Seconds

The user of the “cursed manipulation” technique utilizes two human souls, molded in the shape of a hammer, to attack their opponent twice.

[2] - Soul Fire

Blockable, Bypasses Ragdoll, Cancellable

Cooldown: 12 Seconds

The “cursed manipulation” user fires several transfigured humans out of their arm, piercing the opponent.

[3] - Focus Strike

Unblockable, Bypasses Ragdoll, Cancellable

Cooldown: 12 Seconds

The “cursed manipulation” technique user imbues cursed energy into their fist to lunge forward and heavily strike their opponent.

[3] (Alt.) - Black Flash

Unblockable, Bypasses Ragdoll, Cancellable

Cooldown: 12 Seconds

Keybind: 3 + 3 (right before you initially strike the opponent with the move: Focus Strike, press 3 again to initiate a black flash. make sure to time it right.)

A black flash can be achieved by anyone who possesses cursed energy in the jujutsu world. The user applies their cursed energy 1.0E-6 seconds before they hit the opponent, creating a distortion of space and amplifying the destructive power of their punch.
